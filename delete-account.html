Delete Your PayIQ Account

You can delete your PayIQ account and all associated data directly from within the app.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Open the PayIQ app
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm when prompted

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your profile information (name, NHS band, pay settings)
  • All shift records

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be immediately and permanently deleted.

Need help?

If you are unable to delete your account from within the app, contact us at support@payiq.co.uk
